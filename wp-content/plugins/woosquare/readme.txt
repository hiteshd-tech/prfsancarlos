== Why Should You Install WC Shop Sync? — 5 Key Reasons ==

Managing your online store can be challenging, especially when using multiple platforms (i.e., **Square and WooCommerce**). An inaccurate stock count often leads to overselling, stockouts, and frustrated customers.

But not anymore! 🎉😀

WC Shop Sync solves this problem by connecting Square with WooCommerce so that your inventory, orders, and customer data stay in sync.

Here are four key reasons to install WC Shop Sync today!

⚡ **Reason #1: Add Multiple Payment Options for WooCommerce**

[According to Baymard Institute](https://baymard.com/lists/cart-abandonment-rate#:~:text=10%25,enough%20payment%20methods), 10% of customers will abandon their shopping carts if their preferred payment methods aren't available.

But don’t worry! With WC Shop Sync, you can easily integrate Square Payment Gateway with your WooCommerce store, so besides just debit and credit cards, your customers can pay with  **Google Pay, [Apple Pay](https://apiexperts.io/blog/apple-pay-in-woocommerce/), ACH payments, and Square Gift Cards**.
 
Having this flexibility with Square Gateway enables you to provide a smooth and convenient checkout experience for your customers.

⚡ **Reason #2: 1-Click Inventory Sync Between Square and WooCommerce**

As you know, manual inventory updates are time-consuming and prone to errors. 

According to a study by [Wasp Barcode Technologies](https://www.waspbarcode.com/buzz/5-reasons-why-small-businesses-need-inventory-cloud), 43% of small businesses manually manage inventory, meaning they either do not track inventory at all or rely heavily on manual processes.

To make inventory sync management easier, WC Shop Sync offers a **1-click** [inventory sync between Square and WooCommerce](https://apiexperts.io/blog/sync-inventory-between-square-and-woocommerce-stores/). 🎉

This way, you will always be sure that your stock levels are always up-to-date, thus significantly reducing the risk of overselling or stockouts.

⚡ **Reason #3: Real-time Orders and Customer Data Synchronization**

Are you having trouble tracking orders and customer data between your Square and WooCommerce stores?

Worry no more! 🙂

WC Shop Sync empowers you to synchronize orders and customer information between Square and WooCommerce in real time. This helps streamline your order management and improve customer service.

⚡ **Reason #4: Detailed Activity Log of Each Inventory Sync Coming Soon**

Monitoring activities such as product sync, orders, customer data, etc., helps maintain transparency and accountability. However, keeping track of all these changes can be a nightmare, especially when you are managing a large inventory. 😣

WC Shop Sync’s upcoming feature, a **detailed activity log screen**, will record every sync action. This will enable you to quickly monitor changes and resolve discrepancies, maintaining smooth operations and accurate inventory management between Square and WooCommerce.

⚡ **Reason #5: Boost Repeat Sales with Square Loyalty Sync**

Returning customers spend up to 5x as much as new customers. With Square Loyalty support in WC Shop Sync, shoppers can earn and redeem loyalty points in your store. When customers have points available, a simple checkbox lets them redeem rewards instantly, and the discount is applied to their order total in real time.
Since loyalty activity syncs automatically between WooCommerce and Square, you never have to track rewards manually. No more hurdles, just more repeat purchases!
